Story of This Serie

STORY

The series "Omar" is an Arabic historical drama
produced by Good News for Media and directed Hatem Ali.
It narrates the life story of the caliph Omar ibn al-Khattab,
who is considered one of the greatest leaders in Islamic history.
The series consists of 30 episodes and reflects various aspects of Omar ibn al-Khattab's life, from the pre-Islamic period to his caliphate.
The show portrays Omar ibn al-Khattab as a just and strong ruler and highlights how he implemented principles of justice and equality.The series features a group of prominent Arab actors, including Samer al-Masri in the role of Omar ibn al-Khattab, a performance that received widespread acclaim. Many well-known actors with historical roles also participate in the project. The series has received positive reactions from both audiences and critics, with praise given for its performance, writing, and direction. This has contributed to enhancing people's understanding of Omar ibn al-Khattab's character and history.
